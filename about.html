What is Tender Conflict?

Hey man, I'm Tender Conflict. My purpose in this world is to make art, music, and to make people happy.

I live for nothing more than to help the people I meet irl and online. Music has always been a passion of mine, I had always played piano when I was younger but I never composed anything until I started using Reaper on my laptop with some video game soundfonts downloaded. It was December of 2021 in college. I figured with another suicide date set I might as well try making something creative. Even with crappy small songs I made, The fulfilment it gave me kept me going. I want to make something better, and better, and better and better and better. I never want to stop creating art and music. No matter what it takes.

Some time after moving back into my parent's house I was able to come up with a song that I was finally proud of called "Drills". I spent some time after that coming up with a name, It took a few days but I ended up with the name Tender Conflict and was happy with it and how it looked. I eventually made more and then created my first album during that summer of 2022.

I hope I can keep continuing to make music that makes you smile, and art that makes you smile. Im currently working on my own little indie game, and I have been blessed by God to compose music for a great platforming indie game. I strive to try my hardest to make something with soul, something meaningful.

With the support of my friends, my wonderful mother, my beautiful Wife, and you I have been able to keep on going and bring more music into this world. Ps: Trans rights forever
